Comprehensive Rivet Solutions

Engineered for excellence, manufactured for reliability. Discover our complete range of high-performance rivets designed for every industrial application.

Blind Rivets

High-performance rivets for applications where only one side of the joint is accessible. Perfect for automotive, HVAC, and electrical enclosures with superior vibration resistance.

Easy Installation Vibration Resistant Strong Joints
Material: Aluminium, Steel, SS
Diameter: 2.4mm - 6.4mm

POP Rivets

Also known as pull rivets, these offer excellent gripping strength and easy installation. Ideal for sheet metal applications and light assembly work across multiple industries.

Strong Grip Quick Install Quick Install Versatile
Head Style: Dome, Flat, Countersunk
Length: 6mm - 25mm

Aluminium Blind Rivets

Corrosion-resistant with excellent strength-to-weight ratio. Widely used in construction, HVAC installations, and signage applications requiring lightweight solutions.

Lightweight Corrosion Resistant Durable
Finish: Natural, Anodized
Grip Range: 0.5mm - 12mm

Aluminium POP Rivets

Combining lightweight design with non-corrosive properties. Used in applications where both functionality and appearance matter, offering superior aesthetics.

Aesthetic Non-Corrosive Functional
Colors: Natural, Black, White
Applications: Architectural, Marine

Aluminium Solid Rivets

The preferred choice where maximum strength is crucial. Used in high-load and structural applications for permanent fastening with uncompromising reliability.

Maximum Strength Structural Permanent
Strength: Up to 350 MPa
Standards: DIN 660, ISO 15977
