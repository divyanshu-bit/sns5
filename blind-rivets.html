Blind Rivets

POP rivets, sometimes referred to as blind rivets, are fasteners made for applications where it is impossible to reach the back side of a joint. They are used to swiftly and securely join materials from one side, forming a solid, impenetrable grip that remains intact even when pressured or vibrated.
