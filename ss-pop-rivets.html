SS POP Rivets

Stainless steel blind or "SS POP" rivets are important fasteners designed to quickly and safely connect different materials, particularly when one side of the connection is not accessible. SS POP rivets provide a strong, permanent bond without needing welding or threading, known for their exceptional strength and resistance to corrosion.
